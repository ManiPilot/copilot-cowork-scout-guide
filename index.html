Three products, three jobs to be done

Each product is optimized for a distinct type of work. Understanding what each is built for makes choosing between them straightforward.

Microsoft Copilot

Your in-flow AI assistant

Embedded intelligence inside the Microsoft 365 apps you use every day.

Best for

  • Individual productivity inside Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, and Teams
  • Drafting, summarizing, formatting, and rewriting content
  • Meeting recaps and email triage
  • Data analysis and formulas in Excel
Use it when

You're working inside a single M365 app and want AI to help with the task in front of you.

Microsoft Cowork

Your team's shared AI workspace

A collaborative surface where teams and AI agents co-create work in real time.

Best for

  • Multi-person projects with shared context and artifacts
  • Team brainstorms, planning sessions, and reviews with AI in the loop
  • Persistent workspaces where humans and agents collaborate over days or weeks
  • Cross-functional work needing shared memory and decisions
Use it when

Multiple people plus AI need to work on the same thing over time with shared context.

Microsoft Scout

Your agentic desktop operator

A capable AI assistant that takes action across your files, browser, terminal, and M365 data.

Best for

  • Multi-step tasks that span apps, files, and the web
  • Building artifacts: sites, decks, docs, spreadsheets, code
  • Automating workflows, running scripts, and orchestrating tools
  • Deep research and cross-referencing across your work data
Use it when

You need an agent to do something complex end-to-end, not just help you write.

The Copilot agent ecosystem

Three ways to get agents to work: use ones Microsoft ships, add ones from partners in the marketplace, or build your own. All plug into the Copilot experience.

How this relates to Copilot / Cowork / Scout: Agents from any source live in the Copilot ecosystem. Teams can collaborate around them in Cowork, and Scout can invoke and orchestrate them alongside its own multi-step actions.
