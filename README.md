# Copilot vs. Cowork vs. Scout — Customer Guide

A single-page interactive site helping customers choose the right Microsoft AI experience for their scenario.

**Live site:** https://manipilot.github.io/copilot-cowork-scout-guide/

## What's inside

- **Product overview** — Copilot, Cowork, and Scout side-by-side
- **Decision framework** — three questions that point to the right product
- **Interactive scenario picker** — recommends a product from three quick answers
- **Common use cases** — filterable by product
- **The Copilot agent ecosystem** — Microsoft 1st-party agents, partner agents, and custom-built patterns (Agent Builder, Copilot Studio, Azure AI Foundry)
- **Feature comparison matrix**

## Local preview

Just open `index.html` in a browser — it's a self-contained single-file site.
